Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days within Sri Lanka. Express delivery (2-3 days) is also available.

How do custom tailoring orders work?

Contact us with your requirements, share your measurements, choose fabric and design. We'll craft your garment and deliver it within 7-14 days.

What's your return policy?

Ready-made items can be returned within 7 days if unused. Custom tailored pieces include one free alteration.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, cash on delivery, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely.
